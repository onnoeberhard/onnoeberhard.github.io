About

This is the blog of Onno Eberhard (like it says in large, friendly letters on the left side).

Onno currently studies Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen. Before that, he studied Electrical Engineering at the University of Duisburg-Essen.

His main research interest lies in Reinforcement Learning, though he is also interested in all aspects of Theoretical Computer Science, Physics and Mathematics.

He spends most of his free time being a huge nerd (cf. Figure 1), though he recently also found a passion in writing texts about himself in the third person.
